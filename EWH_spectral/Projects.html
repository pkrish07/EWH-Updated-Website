Wheelchair Repair and Innovation Project

in collaboration with University of Makerere ( in progress) Wheelchairs acquired from local organizations will repaired and redesigned to be more adaptable on uneven terrain. Wheelchairs will then be sent to the EWH chapter at the University of Makerere in Uganda to be distributed in local communities.
